A Day of Art, Joy & Healing

Our team visited the Divine Touch Orphanage to engage children with disabilities through hands-on art activities, music, and storytelling. The goal was to create a safe and fun environment for creative expression, connection, and emotional healing.

Through creative engagement, these children found new ways to express themselves — and we found new reasons to believe in the healing power of art.
